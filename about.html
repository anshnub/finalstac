About Us

At stac., we explore the interplay of form, function, and feeling—creating spaces that are sculptural yet intuitive, expressive yet timeless.
Through our work, we invite people to engage with architecture in a way that is both personal and profound—where every space tells a story, and every detail carries meaning.


Founded in 2024, stac. is an architecture and interior design studio driven by the seamless fusion of art and logic. Rooted in the belief that spaces have the power to evoke emotion, spark curiosity, and shape experiences, our designs are crafted with intention, depth, and storytelling.
